# Finance & Risk Analytics Portfolio

Welcome to my portfolio. This repository contains advanced financial modeling projects built in Microsoft Excel, focusing on credit risk evaluation and market stress-testing frameworks. These projects demonstrate my practical application of quantitative finance and risk management principles.

---

## Project 1: Counterparty Credit Analysis Model
**Objective:** Developed a comprehensive credit assessment model to evaluate corporate borrower default risk and assign risk ratings.

### Key Features:
* **Financial Statement Analysis:** Integrated 3 years of historical balance sheets, income statements, and cash flow statements.
* **Ratio Calculations:** Automated key credit metrics including Liquidity (Current/Quick ratios), Leverage (Debt-to-Equity, Interest Coverage), and Profitability (ROCE, Net Margin).
* **Risk Rating Engine:** Built a weighted scoring matrix that translates financial health and qualitative factors into a standardized risk rating.

### Tech/Functions Used:
* `VLOOKUP`, `INDEX / MATCH`, `IFERROR`, Conditional Formatting, Financial Ratio Analysis.

## Project 2: Portfolio Stress Testing Framework
**Objective:** Constructed a scenario-based stress testing model to simulate historical and hypothetical market shocks on asset valuations.

### Key Features:
* **Macroeconomic Scenarios:** Modeled specific market shocks including severe interest rate hikes (+200 bps), equity market drawdowns (-25%), and currency fluctuations.
* **Sensitivity Analysis:** Isolated specific portfolio variables to determine which assets carry the highest concentration of market risk.
* **Dynamic Dashboard:** Built an interactive summary view allowing stakeholders to toggle between "Baseline", "Moderate Stress", and "Severe Crash" scenarios.

### Tech/Functions Used:
* Excel Data Tables, Data Analysis Toolpak, `SUMPRODUCT`, `OFFSET`, Dynamic Charts.
